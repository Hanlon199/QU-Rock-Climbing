What is the Climbing Club?

Good Question! The simplest answer is that the climbing club is a loosely organized group of fellow adventurers at Quinnipiac University who enjoy to climb all around! This includes bouldering, sport climbing, trad climbing and even other outdoor activity that we think is fun.

Our primary mission is to get climbers of all skill levels interacting with one another. Whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned vet, the QU Rock Climbing Club is a great resource to meet fellow enthusiasts!
